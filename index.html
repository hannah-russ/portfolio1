Phase 1 Course Goals


1. Foster the creative habits of mind

Starting this course, I did not know anything about coding, which I quickly learned is okay because I have other assets that can help me excel in this class. I am an Architecture majorso I know how to design for a soecific audience and I think that is very important considering the final project. I have used my creative mind to outside of the box with my last couple projects and have been very proud of the work that I have done. I felt as though the first project was just to get to know the software, but I started feeling more creative in the last two projects.

2. Engage with others in a workshop environment.

It is very intimidating coming into a classroom of different majors, but I figured out that through these differnt lenses, in can bring a new sense of creativity. Working out problems with people before class starts has really helped me connect with people I would have not have connected with before. My classmates have been able to help me with my HTML problems, but have also inspired me to try new, crazy, weird things that I would not have thought about before.

3. Think deeply about craft.

Craft is somthing that is a very big deal within my major and I find it very important when showcasing and the design process. Without craft, it may be hard to understand an idea or get the intended idea across. Through this class and the projects within, I have found how to try new things with the thought of craft in mind. Craft in this case is making sure everything is set the way that is easiest to understand, but still reaching your main goal/presentation.

4. Engage in sustained research and skills development.

Like I said I knew nothing about coding at all before this class, I would even say it intimidated me a lot. The projects have changed my perspective on being confident about it. Through the internet and through our readings, I have researched on how coding works and the ins and outs of it. I have also developed this "hacker" skill that I did not know that I could do before.

5. Learn to present your work

This class has prepared me to be successful in presenting my work, by learning how to "hack" what I want to be done. I have hacked through the HTML to present my work through this portfolio. I now know how to get the effects I want, but still have a lot left to learn!
